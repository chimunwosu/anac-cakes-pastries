Who We Are

About ANAC

ANAC Cake Pastries is a premier bakery and event services company based in Owerri, Imo State, Nigeria. Founded in 2012, we began as a passionate home baking venture and have grown over more than a decade into one of the region's most trusted names in cakes, pastries, catering, and event decoration.

Our journey is a testament to dedication, creativity, and an unwavering commitment to quality. What started with humble beginnings in a home kitchen has blossomed into a full-scale operation serving a wide and diverse clientele — from individual customers and families to corporate institutions, government bodies, and celebrities.

Where It All Began

Our Story

ANAC Cake Pastries was born in 2012 from a simple love of baking. What began as a small home-based operation quickly gained a reputation for outstanding quality and creativity. Word spread, orders grew, and our team expanded.

"Our story is one of ambition, hard work, and heart — and we are just getting started."

Today, more than a decade later, we are proud to operate as a large, professional organisation with a skilled and passionate team dedicated to excellence. From humble beginnings in a home kitchen to serving celebrities, corporate institutions, and government bodies — every cake tells our story of relentless growth.
